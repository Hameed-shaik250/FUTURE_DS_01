# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Introduction
This report analyzes customer behavior across funnel stages to understand conversion and churn.

## Funnel Stages
- Awareness
- Interest
- Consideration
- Conversion

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month users churn more
- Higher charges = higher churn
- Long-term users stay more

## Recommendations
- Offer long-term discounts
- Improve onboarding
- Target high-risk customers

## Conclusion
Better pricing and retention strategies can improve conversions and reduce churn.
